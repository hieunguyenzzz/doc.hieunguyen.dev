# We're running some rule about price

## Price raise on CBM

Price which have CBM less than 0.3 and weight more than 29kg will be raised by 40%

## Price raise on store

All stores apart from UK will have price increase by 15% apart from UK store which being increased by 5%

## Price raise on warehouse container

Price of BVH container will be increase by 10%

## Custom price raise by Product Name `Artichoke`

Artichoke lamp's price will be increase by 10%
